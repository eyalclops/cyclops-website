AI-Powered Data Lake

Cyclops thrives to harness AI at every layer — from integrations to insights — giving SecOps teams the ability to see the unseen and act with confidence.

Integrations, Built Smarter with AI

Traditional security tools rely on rigid connectors that break and lag behind. Cyclops uses AI to rapidly create and maintain integrations with your existing tools — EDR, IAM, MDM, cloud, network, and more. That means faster onboarding, lower maintenance, and always-on compatibility.

Normalized. Correlated. Intelligent.

Data without context is noise. Our AI-driven data lake ingests telemetry from across your stack, then normalizes and correlates every entity — users, devices, roles, policies. This creates a unified security graph that exposes hidden relationships and risks your siloed tools miss.

Answers, Not Dashboards

Cyclops AI Agents let your team ask complex, cross-domain questions in plain language and get clear answers. From "Which unmanaged endpoints belong to admins with outdated credentials?" to "Show me all systems exposed by a legacy policy" — our agents surface insights no single tool can provide.
