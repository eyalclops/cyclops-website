Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2022

This policy applies to our website and mobile website.

What are Cookies?

"A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer"

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

  • Essential for login, navigation, and website features
  • Used for security and integrity
  • Do not require consent

2. Functionality Cookies

  • Remember user choices (like language)
  • Provide personalized features

3. Performance Cookies

  • Collect information about online activity
  • Used for analytics and statistics

4. Marketing/Advertising Cookies

  • Deliver tailored offers and advertisements
  • Placed by advertisers
  • Provide insights about user interactions

Managing Cookies

Users can block cookies through browser settings, but this may limit website access. "If you use your browser settings to block all cookies... you may not be able to access all or parts of our website."
